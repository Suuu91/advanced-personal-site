Bunnies are small mammals that are known for their long ears and fluffy tails. They are often kept as pets and are known for their playful and curious nature. Bunnies are herbivores which means they only eat plants like grass fruits and vegetables. It's important to provide them with a balanced diet to keep them healthy. Overall bunnies make great companions for those who are willing to give them the care and attention they need.
